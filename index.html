Introduction to WhitestormJS
Modern WebGL engine based on Three.js technology, including physics and post effects.

WhitestormJS is based on the Three.js engine, which is made for developers who need a useful wrapper around WebGL, posteffects and physics.

What is the hardest step in programming? - planning(structure). So if you don't want to waste your time including hundreds of libraries to get results, just download and install our latest version.

Installation instructions can be found here.
